Tacos

Description

Serving tacos is a guaranteed success! Many small bowls are filled with delicious treats - spicy minced meat, chopped vegetables, cool sour cream, luxurious guacamole, and more. Let everyone at the table fill their taco with their favorites. More taco fiesta for everyone!

Ingredients

Taco meat

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 tbsp butter & rapeseed oil
  • 1 pack taco seasoning mix
  • 1 dcl water

For serving

  • 150g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 sliced cucumber
  • 1 sliced red onion
  • 2dcl corn kernels
  • 150g grated tex-mex cheese
  • 3dcl sour cream
  • 1 jar tomato salsa
  • 8 tortillas
  • 2dcl guacamole
  • nacho chips
  • fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Fry the ground beef in butter and rapeseed oil until it's crumbly and well-cooked. Add the taco seasoning mix and water. Simmer until the water has evaporated. Remove from heat.
  2. Serve with all the accompaniments arranged in bowls. You can also saut√© the corn in a bit of butter and rapeseed oil for a nice color.
